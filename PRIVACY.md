# Privacy Policy — FastNote Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Summary

Not Defteri does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All notes are stored locally on your device using the Chrome browser's built-in storage API.

## Data Storage

- **What is stored:** Note content (title, body, timestamps, pin status) and your theme preference (light/dark).
- **Where it is stored:** Exclusively in `chrome.storage.sync` and `chrome.storage.local` — both are browser-local APIs provided by Chrome. No external servers are involved.
- **Sync behavior:** `chrome.storage.sync` may sync your notes across devices where you are signed into the same Google account. This sync is handled entirely by Chrome/Google and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
- We do not transmit any data to external servers.
- We do not access your browsing history, tabs, or any webpage content.

## Permissions

The extension requests only the `storage` permission, which is used solely to save and retrieve your notes within the browser.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/furblood0/not-defteri-chrome-extension).
